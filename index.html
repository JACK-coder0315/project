Reflection and Analysis

The first two visualizations support the claim by showing that most complaints are unsubstantiated city-wide and within high-volume precincts. In contrast, the opposing visualizations emphasize spatial coverage and the volume of substantiated complaints.

Personally, I find the second set (B1 and B2) more persuasive. The green color palette and geographic framing convey an image of active response and accountability.

Deceptive techniques used:
- Color symbolism: red = danger/failure, green = action/success
- Proportional vs. absolute emphasis: A1/A2 focus on ratio; B2 on total volume
- Geographic boundaries in B1 frame substantiation positively by omitting unsubstantiated context
